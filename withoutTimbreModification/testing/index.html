Hello and welcome to the auditory streaming experiment!


In this experiment, you will be presented with a series of short melodic patterns in the form of a grid sequencer:

Each box you see in this grid is a note within a melody. Each note has a certain pitch (y-axis, with notes that are higher up indicating a higher pitch) and occurs at a certain point in time (x-axis). The green boxes in the grid are the notes which are actually played when the sequencer is running. The blue box(es) are those which are currently being played at a given point in time.
